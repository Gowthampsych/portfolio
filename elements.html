July 2022 - September 2022
.

As a Game Tester, I was responsible for testing the game for bugs and glitches. During my time at K12 Techno Services Pvt Ltd, I had the opportunity to work as a Game Tester Intern. As part of my role, I was responsible for testing various games and identifying any bugs or issues that needed to be addressed before release. I worked closely with the development team to ensure that the games met the company's high standards for quality and user experience. This experience taught me the importance of attention to detail, effective communication, and the value of teamwork in achieving project success.

Skills :

  • Attention to detail: Being able to spot even the smallest bugs or issues in a game is crucial for a game tester.
  • Problem-solving: Game testers often need to troubleshoot issues and think critically to determine the root cause of a problem.
  • Communication: Being able to clearly communicate issues or bugs found in a game to developers is essential in ensuring they can be fixed efficiently.
  • Patience: Testing a game can be a repetitive process, and it may take several rounds of testing to find all the bugs.
  • Technical skills: Familiarity with gaming platforms, consoles, and software tools used in game development could be helpful in this role
  • Teamwork: Collaboration and working with other testers and developers to resolve issues and improve the overall game experience can be an important aspect of the role
